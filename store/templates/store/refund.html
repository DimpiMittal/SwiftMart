Frequently Asked Questions

1. How long do I have to return an item?

Most items can be returned within 30 days of delivery. However, some products may have different return policies based on the seller.

2. How do I return an item?

Go to 'View Orders', select the item you want to return, and choose the return method. Follow the instructions to complete the process.

3. When will I get my refund?

Refunds are processed within 7–10 business days after the return is received and verified.

4. Can I exchange an item instead of returning it?

Yes, some items are eligible for exchange instead of return. Check the product details for more information.
