Frequently Asked Questions

1. What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept all major credit and debit cards, PayPal, Google Pay, Apple Pay, and UPI. You can also choose "Cash on Delivery" for selected locations.
2. How long does shipping take? +
Shipping times depend on your location. Standard delivery takes 5-7 business days, while express shipping takes 2-3 business days. International orders may take 10-15 days.
Do you offer international shipping? +
No, currently we are available in india only. But is expanding our business, so will soon start international shipping.
3. What is your return and refund policy? +
We offer a 30-day return policy for unused items in their original packaging. Refunds are processed within 7-10 business days after the return is approved.
Can I change my shipping address after placing an order? +
Yes, but you need to contact support within 12 hours of placing the order.
