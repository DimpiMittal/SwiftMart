Orders

Track your orders and check order status.

You can track your orders by visiting the Orders page and entering your order ID. You will get real-time updates on your order status, including expected delivery dates and shipping partner details.

If your order is delayed, please check for any notifications from us regarding unforeseen shipping issues.

Returns & Refunds

Return items and get refunds easily.

To return an item, go to the Returns page and initiate a return request. Ensure the item is in its original packaging and meets our return policy criteria.

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after the returned product passes our quality check.

If your refund is delayed, check your bank account or payment method details and contact us for further assistance.

Payments

Learn about accepted payment methods and troubleshooting.

We accept various payment methods, including:

  • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, RuPay, etc.)
  • UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, etc.)
  • Net Banking (All major banks supported)
  • Wallets (Amazon Pay, Paytm, Mobikwik, etc.)
  • Cash on Delivery (COD) available for select locations

If your payment fails, ensure you have sufficient balance, check your internet connection, or try another payment method.

Account Settings

Manage your profile and security settings.

To update your profile, visit the Profile section in your account.

You can change your email, phone number, address, and password from the settings menu.

For enhanced security, enable two-factor authentication (2FA) in your account settings.

Shipping

Know about delivery timelines and shipping charges.

Orders are generally delivered within 3-5 business days. Remote locations may take up to 7 days.

Shipping charges depend on the product and location. Free shipping is available on orders above ₹999.

For faster delivery, opt for our Express Shipping service at an additional cost.
